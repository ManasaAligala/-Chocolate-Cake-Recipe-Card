Chocolate Cake 🍫

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Eggs
  • Cocoa powder
  • Milk
  • Vegetable oil or Butter
  • Vanilla essence
  • Hot water
  • Chocolate chips
  • Whipped cream
  • Chocolate ganache or frosting
  • Sprinkles

Steps to Prepare

  • Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F)
  • Grease a baking pan
  • Mix flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, salt
  • Mix eggs, milk, oil, vanilla in another bowl
  • Combine wet + dry ingredients → smooth batter
  • Add ½ cup hot water/coffee (optional)
  • Pour into pan
  • Bake for 30–35 mins
  • Cool the cake
  • Add frosting/cream → Serve 🎂
